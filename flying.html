About Me

I have flown over 60 aircraft types to date, from 40hp Piper Cubs to the 2,100shp Beechcraft Super KingAir,

and I hold a range of FAA pilot certifications as a Commercial Pilot and Certifid Flight Instructor:

Flight Instructor - Airplane Single and Multi Engine, Instrument Airplane

Commercial - ASEL, ASES, AMEL, Glider, Instrument Airplane

Ground Instructor - Advanced, Instrument

Remote Pilot - sUAS


I hold FAA Certified Flight Instructor ratings in single and multi-engine airplanes, including instrument instruction,

and Commercial ratings for Airplane Single Engine Land, Airplane Single Engine Sea, Airplane Multiengine Land,

Glider, and Instrument Airplane.  I also hold Tailwheel, High-Performance, Complex, and Glider Tow endorsements.


I value curiosity, insight, and continual learning, so my aim in teaching is always to guide my students

toward mastery, and never to lecture by rote.  I am available to give instruction in your aircraft

or through a number of Bay Area flight schools, as well as for aircraft ferry or as a safety pilot or co-pilot.
